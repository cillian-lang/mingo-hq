# MINGO vs Legacy Ticketing — Side by Side

*Compiled: 2026-02-12 | Quick reference for sales conversations*

---

## THE COMPARISON

### Fees
- Legacy: 15-27% of ticket price
- MINGO: 3.5% flat
- Winner: MINGO (saves 77-87%)

### Ticket Fraud
- Legacy: 12% of U.S. buyers sold fakes (centralized databases can be manipulated)
- MINGO: 0% — every ticket is a cryptographically unique NFT on Hedera, impossible to fake
- Winner: MINGO

### Scalping
- Legacy: No controls. Bots buy thousands in seconds. Resale at 3-10x markup.
- MINGO: Organizer-set resale price caps enforced by smart contract. Can't be bypassed.
- Winner: MINGO

### Resale Revenue
- Legacy: $6.58B secondary market. Artists/organizers earn $0 from resales.
- MINGO: Automatic royalties on every resale. Enforced on-chain. Every time.
- Winner: MINGO

### Speed at the Gate
- Legacy: Varies. Some systems slow. Barcode scanning.
- MINGO: Sub-second verification on Hedera. QR code scan.
- Winner: MINGO

### Setup Time
- Legacy: Contracts, onboarding, account managers. Days to weeks.
- MINGO: Email signup, create event, go live. ~5 minutes.
- Winner: MINGO

### Contracts
- Legacy: Long-term contracts, exclusivity deals, minimum volumes
- MINGO: No contracts, no minimums, no exclusivity. Use for one event or a thousand.
- Winner: MINGO

### Technology
- Legacy: Centralized databases. Single point of failure.
- MINGO: Decentralized on Hedera (governed by Google, IBM, Boeing). Tamper-proof.
- Winner: MINGO

### Environmental Impact
- Legacy: Standard server infrastructure
- MINGO: Built on Hedera — carbon negative network
- Winner: MINGO

### User Experience
- Legacy: Often requires app download. Varies by platform.
- MINGO: PWA — works in any browser, any phone. No download.
- Winner: Tie (both functional, MINGO slightly better for no-download)

### Discovery / Marketplace
- Legacy: Built-in audience. Fans browse and find events organically.
- MINGO: No consumer marketplace yet. Organizers bring their own audience.
- Winner: Legacy

### Reserved Seating
- Legacy: Full seat map support. Assigned seating.
- MINGO: Not yet available. General admission only.
- Winner: Legacy

### Marketing Tools
- Legacy: Email campaigns, CRM, retargeting, audience insights, promo codes
- MINGO: Basic event management. Advanced marketing tools in development.
- Winner: Legacy

### Box Office / Door Sales
- Legacy: POS systems, box office integration, cash handling
- MINGO: Digital-only currently
- Winner: Legacy

### Brand Awareness
- Legacy: Household names. Decades of market presence.
- MINGO: Growing. 10.3K followers. Strong in Hedera ecosystem.
- Winner: Legacy

### API / Integrations
- Legacy: Extensive APIs. Integrations with hundreds of tools.
- MINGO: Limited API currently.
- Winner: Legacy

## SCORE SUMMARY

**MINGO wins on:** Fees, fraud prevention, anti-scalping, resale revenue, gate speed, setup time, contracts, technology, environmental impact (9 categories)

**Legacy wins on:** Discovery marketplace, reserved seating, marketing tools, box office, brand awareness, API/integrations (6 categories)

**Key insight:** MINGO wins on everything that matters for the TICKET (cost, security, fairness). Legacy wins on everything around the ticket (discovery, marketing, tools). This is MINGO's development roadmap in a nutshell — close the tooling gap while maintaining the core advantages.
